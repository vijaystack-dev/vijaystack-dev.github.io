About me

I’m a Machine Learning Engineer driven by a passion for building intelligent systems that understand and interact with the world like humans do. My work spans across Speech AI, Generative AI, and Large Language Models (LLMs) — with a focus on real-world impact and research-backed innovation.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on a wide range of deep learning applications, including zero-shot voice conversion, expressive text-to-speech, and multimodal AI. One of my most impactful projects is Dis-Vector, a voice embedding framework designed to capture the essence of a speaker's identity from just a few seconds of audio. It powers voice cloning that’s not only accurate, but expressive and human-like.

I enjoy working at the intersection of cutting-edge research and practical deployment. Whether it’s refining LLMs for language understanding or building speech synthesis pipelines, I’m always exploring how to bring AI closer to human communication.

Beyond the technical side, I value meaningful collaboration, continuous learning, and building things that matter. If you're working on something exciting in speech tech, generative models, or AI-powered creativity — let’s connect.
